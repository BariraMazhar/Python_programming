For Example:

-------------------
Arithmetic operator
-------------------
	1-Adddition: (a+b)
		!0+2=12
	
	2-Subtraction: (a-b)
		10-2=8

	3-Multiplication: (*)
		10*2=20

	4-Divison: (/)
		10/2=5

	5-Modulus: (%)
		*This is used to print the remainder.
		10%2=0
